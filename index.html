Emotion Translator

😀 Welcome

😂 😔 😡 Neurotypical people often communicate their thoughts and emotions through their facial expressions. This AI model can provide some suggestions on what a neurotypical person may be feeling based on an image or live video of their face.

🧐 Remember, expressions can vary across cultures, individuals and contexts, and AI models also struggle to reliably identify feelings. This AI can only provide a rough estimate. If it feels right to you, you can consider asking, "I'm sensing you are feeling [insert emotion]. Would you be comfortable talking about that?"

Neurotypical Responses

To use this model, first ask if the other person is comfortable sharing their face on your
camera, with your AI model. If they say yes, ask them to move into the camera frame.
